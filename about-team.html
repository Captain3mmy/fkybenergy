CEO

Oyeleke Adewale Moses

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Mr Oyeleke is a graduate of Ahmadu Bello University with a Masters degree in Architecture, a business executive with over 25 years’ experience, he is the founder of Probuilders Limited an indigenous construction company with several notable projects under its belt. He has advised and worked on several construction projects across Nigeria. After he completed his youth service, he began his career with a construction firm in Lagos, and then started Probuilders Limited.

In 2008, he started FKYB Energy Limited due to a partnership with a German friend to supply diesel into Nigeria as there were no operating refineries in Nigeria at the time. This partnership extended to Indonesia with a contract to supply them with crude oil. He is the founder of companies such as FKYB Energy, Adexport and Leks Investment. The companies and projects are to the tune of billions of naira, he lives a modest life and is blessed with children

 

FKYB Energy continues to grow everday thanks to the confidence our clients have in us. We cover many industries such as oil, gas, energy, business services and consumer products.

COO

Oyeleke Oluwafolakanmi

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Mr Oyeleke is the founder of Feed Afrique, a sustainable agritech venture into carbon neutral dairy farming and renewable energy production, he is an agriculture, biomass and energy generation expert.He started his career at Probuilders Limited as a site engineer upon graduation from high school , then to I.N.V.K as a sales rep, Segway Go as a Distributor, Megat and Partners as a business development executive, Green Eagles Agribusiness solutions as a business development executive, before starting his first company Fola Homes, then his second Feed Afrique. His experience spans several sectors construction, real estate, FMCG, agriculture, technology and energy. Possesses skills in strategy, marketing, critical thinking and problem solving. He is a deliberate leader. He sits on the board of two family businesses Leks Investment and FKYBOIL Limited, and is currently pursuing a masters degree in Architecture.

 

We are committed to providing cost-effective and safer energy alternative solutions.

CFO

Dr Chukwuma Ucheagwu

Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Dr Chukwuma is a graduate of accounting from Babcock University. He holds a doctorate degree in accounting awarded by Babcock University. Dr. Chukwuma is a fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria, Associate of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers and Chartered Institute of Taxation, Nigeria. He has attended several executive diploma programmes in pension Management, risk management and corporate governance.Dr. Chukwuma started his career as an accounts officer with KGM Industries Limited in Lagos. From there he moved to become the Accountant of Micro Instruments Limited, a computer hardware and software dealing company in Lagos. He is currently the Chief Finance Officer of Valmon Securities Limited, an asset management company in Lagos. His experience spans a period of eighteen (18) years, ten (10) of which is at management position. He has vast experience in accounting, financial engineering, taxation, and asset management. Dr. Chukwuma lectures on Strategic Financial Management and Securities and Portfolio theory at the University of Ibadan Business School. He is a regular facilitator for Olabisi Onabanjo University,Ogun State. He is happily married with children.
